Why Soup dumplings are the Best Food

  1. Tastes delicious

  2. Is filling

  3. Comforting

  4. Easy to make

Soup dumplings are simply the best type of food. Unlike traditional dumplings, they are filled with soup so you have the right ratio between meat and soup. They are warm and comforting, perfect for a cold winter evening.
